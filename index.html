SERVICES DIVISION:

A team of 3 members is always in a ready position to provide all sorts of services required for farming to the farmers. Agricultural loans, subsidies, job work of machinery, KCC services, and many more.

SALES DIVISION:

Apart from all, this division plays a vital role for the farmers. Purchasing crops of small farmers and providing them prices higher than the market is their motto. Small farmers never had this option before but our team procures their crop at their doorstep and gives them their deserving prices. We have installed a 3 layer factory in the industrial area of Jaisalmer, which has WAREHOUSE, PROCESSING, AND PACKAGING of cumin and groundnut.
