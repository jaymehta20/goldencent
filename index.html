    Journey

    Thar city “Jaisalmer” is named after the founder and ruler Rawal Jaiswal Bhati in 1156 AD.

    Jaisalmer city was well known for tourism and it’s scenic beauty, but there was no root connection of farming & agriculture before a historic step by the government i.e “Indira Gandhi Canal Construction” in 1952.

    Jaisalmer from city of dunes and sand soon converted into biggest and largest district to produce crops like Cumin, Groundnut & Isabgol.

    After the canal construction, 1967-1968 was the year which is also known as Green Revolution, changned the image of India in the world map from food deficient country to one of the world’s leading agricultural nation.

    As per rural data, people from different parts and states of India started migrating to Jaisalmer & its nearby area for healthy agricultural practices,

    2003-2004 was considered as the best year for production of major crops like Cumin, Groundnut & Isabgol.

    In year 2020, Goldencent Framer’s Producer Organization targets to reach and spread its wings in every single rural area of Jaisalmer & nearby. As already stated our main moto is “From the farmers, For the farmers, To the farmers” & uplift small & marginal farmers by giving them the real values of their crops and farms.
